Yesterday at Watson West in San Francisco IBM Research unveiled Project Debater, the first AI-powered, computational argumentation tool, at an invitation-only media and influencer event hosted by IBM Research Director Arvind Krishna. To demonstrate how IBM Research is advancing language mastery in AI, two college-level champion debaters engaged Debater in a live debate. More than 50 reporters, analysts and influencers attended the event, with resulting global print and broadcast coverage in: Fortune, the Associated Press, CNET, The Verge, Bloomberg, NBC News, USA TODAY, WIRED, The Financial Times, BBC and many others.

The news was also shared through a variety of top-tier media newsletters, including: The Wall Street Journal CIO Journal, New York Times Morning Brief, The New York Times DealBook, Fortune CEO Daily, Fortune Data Sheet and Axios Login: Tuesday, June 19.
